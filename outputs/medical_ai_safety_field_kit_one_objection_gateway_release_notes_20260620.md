# Medical AI Safety Field Kit One Objection Gateway

This release makes the One Objection Gateway the first public Field Kit route.

Public gateway:

https://github.com/v0id-lab/medical-ai-failure-atlas/blob/main/docs/MEDICAL_AI_SAFETY_FIELD_KIT_ONE_OBJECTION_GATEWAY_20260620.md

Related starter issue:

https://github.com/v0id-lab/medical-ai-failure-atlas/issues/154

The release asks for one narrow objection before medical AI safety wording becomes too trusted.

Useful objection shape:

```text
Role:
Lane:
Risk:
Missing gate:
Safer wording:
```

Boundary:

Use synthetic or public material only. No patient data, private clinical text, raw private model output, diagnosis advice, treatment advice, clinical validation, clinical deployment, benchmark ranking, score certification, source truth certification, partner claim, institution claim, endorsement, formal application, payment, terms action, budget action, procurement claim, or official role claim.
