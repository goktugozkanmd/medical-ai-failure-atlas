# Medical AI Safety Field Kit Global Safety Claim Review Workbook

This release adds the Global Safety Claim Review Workbook for the Medical AI Safety Field Kit.

Public workbook:

https://github.com/v0id-lab/medical-ai-failure-atlas/blob/main/docs/MEDICAL_AI_SAFETY_FIELD_KIT_GLOBAL_SAFETY_CLAIM_REVIEW_WORKBOOK_20260620.md

The workbook unifies:

1. Main public intake issue 149
2. Source support starter issue 150
3. Turkish wording starter issue 151
4. Hospital readiness starter issue 152
5. Safe Failure Card mapping for SFC001 through SFC010
6. Synthetic claim walkthrough
7. Reviewer comment templates
8. Objection counting rule

Boundary:

Synthetic or public examples only. No patient data, clinical validation, clinical deployment, benchmark ranking, score certification, source truth certification, partner claim, institution approval, endorsement, formal application, payment, terms action, TBYS action, PRODIS action, budget action, or procurement claim.
