## NIST AI RMF

Official page:

https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework

Verified source facts:

1. The page is an official NIST page.
2. NIST describes the AI RMF as a framework for managing AI risks to individuals, organizations, and society.
3. NIST states that AI RMF 1.0 is intended for voluntary use.
4. NIST states that the framework supports trustworthiness considerations across design, development, use, and evaluation.
5. The page says AI RMF 1.0 is being revised as of the checked date.

Use in card:

Risk management and trustworthiness orientation only.

Not supported:

NIST endorsement, NIST assessment of MedFailBench, or NIST validation.

Support status: verified for source description only.
