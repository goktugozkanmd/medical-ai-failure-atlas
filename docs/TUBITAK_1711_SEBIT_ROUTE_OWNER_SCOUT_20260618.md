Date: 2026 06 18

Status: route owner scout, not an application.

Purpose: turn the named smart education scout into a direct route owner question for an education technology provider surface.

This packet is not a TÜBİTAK application, not an intent declaration, not a meeting request, not a partner commitment, not a budget claim, and not a claim that SEBİT, Acibadem CASE, TÜYZE, TÜBİTAK, TÜSEB, or any other organization supports this work.

## Scout verdict

The next highest leverage action is to ask SEBİT whether clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education infrastructure is a meaningful smart education technology problem.

Direct health AI fit remains weak for TÜBİTAK 1711 because health is not listed as an official 2026 priority area. The honest route is smart education technologies only.
