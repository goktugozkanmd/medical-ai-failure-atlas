## Public GitHub or LinkedIn call

I am looking for 2 to 4 clinicians to review a small MedFailBench pilot packet.

The packet uses synthetic cases only. Each case receives two independent clinician ratings: severity 1 to 5, likely harm if copied, missing safety gate, failure tags, confidence, and whether the case should stay in the benchmark.

This is research review. It is not clinical advice, not clinical validation, and not a claim that any model is ready for clinical use.

If you can review 10 to 15 cases in about one hour, please comment or open an issue.
