# TÜBİTAK 1711 Smart Education Fit Gate

Date: 2026 06 18

Status: live call fit gate.

Purpose: turn the live TÜBİTAK 1711 Yapay Zeka Ekosistem 2026 call into an actionable but truthful decision gate for clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education infrastructure.

This is not an application, not an intent declaration, not a partner commitment, not a budget claim, not a meeting request, and not a health priority fit claim.

## Current verdict

Direct health AI fit is weak because health is not listed among the official 2026 priority areas.

The only plausible adjacent route found in the official priority list is smart education technologies. A route may be worth scouting only if the proposed work is framed as AI enabled education infrastructure for clinician AI literacy, health AI safety training, source support review training, and human review workflow training.

This gate blocks any 1711 action until an eligible customer organization, technology provider company, and experienced university or public research route are verified.

## Smart education route hypothesis

The only route worth scouting is:

1. Customer organization: a medical faculty, hospital education unit, health informatics program, or continuing medical education body that needs clinician AI literacy infrastructure.
2. Technology provider: an eligible AI or education technology company that can build or integrate the learning platform.
3. Research route: a university research lab, health informatics unit, medical education unit, ethics unit, or public research route experienced enough to support evaluation.
4. Open source sidecar: Medical AI Failure Atlas and SourceCheckup Medical can contribute safety scenarios, source support checks, no ranking failure reports, clinician review protocols, and data quality training materials.

## Fit gates

### Gate 1: priority fit

Pass only if the route is framed as smart education technologies.

Fail if the route is framed as direct health AI, hospital deployment, clinical decision support, model certification, or clinical validation.

### Gate 2: customer need

Pass only if a real customer organization need is identified for clinician AI literacy or health AI safety education.

Fail if the route is only a public repo, personal portfolio, or generic awareness note.

### Gate 3: technology provider

Pass only if an eligible technology provider company is identified.

Fail if there is no company route.

### Gate 4: research route

Pass only if a university lab, center, public research center, institute, or equivalent research route is verified.

Fail if there is no research route.

### Gate 5: formal action boundary

Pass only for scouting, memo drafting, and public readiness artifacts.

Fail for application submission, intent declaration, meeting request, partner commitment, budget claim, terms acceptance, official role claim, endorsement claim, patient data use, clinical deployment claim, or clinical validation claim.

## Immediate field move

If no eligible route is found, shift effort to hospital innovation outreach, TÜYZE aligned education readiness, TÜSEB proposal readiness, or TEKNOFEST health AI safety support.
