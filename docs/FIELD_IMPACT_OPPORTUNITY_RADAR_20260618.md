## Opportunity one: TÜBİTAK 1711 Yapay Zeka Ekosistem 2026

Official source: https://tubitak.gov.tr/tr/duyuru/1711-yapay-zeka-ekosistem-2026-yili-cagrisi-acildi

Current facts checked:

1. The 2026 call opened on 15 June 2026.
2. Applications are received through PRODİS from 15 June 2026 to 18 September 2026 according to the official page.
3. Pre registration must be completed by 14 September 2026 at 17:30 according to the official page.
4. The call lists five priority areas: smart production systems, smart agriculture, food and livestock, financial technologies, climate change and sustainability, and smart education technologies.
5. The official page says a consortium needs at least one technology provider company and at least one experienced university research lab, center, public research center, or institute.
6. The official page says the consortium is expected to meet TÜBİTAK Yapay Zeka Enstitüsü before application and include an intent declaration form.

Health fit:

Direct health fit is weak because health is not listed among the five priority areas. The aggressive but truthful move is not to force a health claim. The strongest route is an AI assurance, data quality, and safety readiness package that can attach to eligible AI domains while preserving a health AI safety portfolio as proof of work.

Prepared impact move:

Build a TÜBİTAK 1711 AI assurance sidecar memo that shows how a customer organization, technology provider, and research lab could use public safety gates, data quality checks, source support review, and no ranking failure reports in any eligible AI project. This is a field package, not a submission.

Decision needed from Dr. Ozkan:

Whether to pursue a 1711 adjacent consortium scouting path despite weak direct health priority fit.

Whether to prepare a direct TÜYZE oriented introduction draft and whether to identify a named academic or institutional route before contact.

Whether to prepare a public GitHub discussion or issue aimed at interoperability with model card style disclosure.
