## OpenAI HealthBench

Official source:

https://openai.com/index/healthbench/

Verified source facts:

1. The page is an OpenAI page for HealthBench.
2. It describes HealthBench as an evaluation for AI systems and human health.
3. It states that HealthBench was built with 262 physicians who practiced in 60 countries.
4. It states that HealthBench includes 5,000 realistic health conversations.
5. It states that the conversations use custom physician created rubrics and 48,562 unique rubric criteria.
