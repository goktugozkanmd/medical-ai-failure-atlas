# Issue 154 public comment router

Date TRT: 2026 06 20 14:04

Public destination:

`https://github.com/v0id-lab/medical-ai-failure-atlas/issues/154`

## Purpose

Convert one outside reaction into one safe public comment without turning the reaction into endorsement, validation, partnership, or clinical evidence.

## Use when

1. A contact says the project wording is unclear.
2. A contact replies privately with a weak spot but no patient data.
3. A contact is willing to comment but does not know what shape is useful.
4. A reviewer wants to flag a source support, synthetic evidence, Turkish wording, readiness wording, or governance wording risk.

## Do not use when

1. The reply contains patient data or private clinical detail.
2. The reply asks for diagnosis, treatment, or clinical advice.
3. The reply suggests clinical validation, deployment, ranking, score certification, endorsement, institutional support, partner status, formal application, payment, or terms action.

## Fast public comment shapes

Use one of these.

```text
Weak spot:
Why it matters:
Safer wording or missing gate:
```

```text
Link or section:
What seems weak, confusing, overstated, or missing:
What would make it clearer or more defensible:
Optional evidence or example:
```

## If the reply is private but safe

Ask the person to post a public version themselves.

```text
This is useful. Could you paste a public version into issue 154 using only synthetic or public wording?

Please do not include patient data, private clinical text, clinical advice, validation, deployment, endorsement, institution, partner, ranking, score, payment, or terms claims.

The public comment can be one sentence:

Weak spot:
Safer wording or missing gate:
```

## If the reply has private clinical detail

Do not copy it into GitHub.

Ask for a synthetic rewrite.

```text
I cannot use private clinical detail in the public issue. If you are willing, please rewrite the point as a synthetic or public wording risk only.

Useful shape:

Weak spot:
Why it matters:
Safer wording or missing gate:
```

## If the reply is praise only

Do not count it.

Ask for one objection.

```text
Thank you. For this project I need a criticism, not endorsement. Could you name one weak wording, missing gate, or unsupported claim?
```

## After the first valid public objection

1. Stop the warm contact sprint.
2. Do not call the commenter a partner, reviewer, validator, collaborator, or institutional supporter.
3. Patch one wording, gate, or boundary.
4. Link the patch or follow up issue.
5. Record only the public comment URL and whether private clinical detail appeared.

## Boundary

Synthetic or public examples only.

No patient data.

No private clinical text.

No clinical advice.

No clinical validation.

No clinical deployment.

No benchmark ranking.

No score certification.

No source truth certification.

No partner claim.

No institution claim.

No endorsement.

No formal application.

No payment.

No terms action.
