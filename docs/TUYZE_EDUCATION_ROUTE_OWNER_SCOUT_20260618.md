# TÜYZE Education Route Owner Scout

Date: 2026 06 18

Status: route owner scout, not an application.

Purpose: ask whether clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education readiness can be reviewed by the correct TÜYZE or TÜSEB education route owner.

This packet is not a TÜBİTAK application, not a TÜSEB proposal, not an intent declaration, not a meeting request, not a partner commitment, not a budget claim, and not a claim that TÜYZE, TÜSEB, TÜBİTAK, SEBİT, Acibadem CASE, or any other organization supports this work.

## Scout verdict

TÜYZE is a high value route owner surface because public pages show health AI education and health AI institutional activity. A general information e mail is suitable for a non commitment route owner question.

This scout is separate from a formal TÜBİTAK 1711 route. Direct health AI fit remains weak for TÜBİTAK 1711 because health is not listed as an official 2026 priority area.

Official source: https://tuyze.tuseb.gov.tr/bilimsel-etkinlikler/egitimler

Checked fact: TÜYZE lists health AI education activities and a health AI seminar series.

Route meaning: clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education is an appropriate topic to ask about as an education readiness question.

Official source: https://tuyze.tuseb.gov.tr/

Checked fact: TÜYZE public pages list health AI seminars, collaboration visits, and institutional health AI activities.

Route meaning: route owner discovery is reasonable, but no support or partnership is implied.

### S003: TÜYZE contact route

Official source: https://tuyze.tuseb.gov.tr/

Checked fact: TÜYZE footer lists TÜSEB İstanbul Yerleşkesi, phone `0 (216) 547 26 00`, general e mail `info@tuseb.gov.tr`, and KEP address `tuseb@hs01.kep.tr`.

Route meaning: use the general e mail for a low risk scout question. Do not use KEP for this non legal scout.

### S004: TÜBİTAK 1711 boundary

Official source: https://tubitak.gov.tr/tr/destekler/sanayi/ulusal-destek-programlari/1711-yapay-zeka-ekosistem-cagrisi

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK 1711 lists smart education technologies among 2026 priority areas and does not list health.

Route meaning: do not frame this as a health priority fit or application route.
