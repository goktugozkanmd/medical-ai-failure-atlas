This packet is not a TÜBİTAK application, not a TÜSEB proposal, not an intent declaration, not a meeting request, not a partner commitment, not a budget claim, and not a claim that TÜBİTAK, TÜSEB, TÜYZE, SEBİT, Acibadem CASE, Hacettepe, or any other organization supports this work.

## Why this exists

TÜBİTAK 1711 is live and nationally important, but health is not listed as a 2026 priority area. The only honest route currently visible is smart education technologies, limited to clinician AI literacy and health AI safety education infrastructure.

The next field impact move should not be another generic document or duplicate e mail. It should clarify whether a credible three gate consortium can exist:

1. Demand side gate.
2. Education technology provider gate.
3. Research route gate.

Until all three gates have verified route owners and source backed fit, this work stays at scouting and readiness level.

Checked fact: TÜBİTAK describes a model where a customer organization, at least one technology provider company, at least one experienced university research laboratory or center or public research center or institute, and TÜBİTAK Yapay Zekâ Enstitüsü form a consortium. Route meaning: a single clinician led open source packet is not enough for 1711. A consortium map is required before any application thinking.
