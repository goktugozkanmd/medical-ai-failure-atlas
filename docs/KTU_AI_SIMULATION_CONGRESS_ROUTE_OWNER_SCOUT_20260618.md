Date: 2026 06 18

Status: route owner scout, not an application.

Purpose: ask whether the health AI safety education, clinician AI literacy, source support review, and no ranking medical AI failure reporting surfaces can be reviewed by the correct Karadeniz Technical University Faculty of Medicine or congress route owner after the 2026 health professionals AI and simulation congress.

This packet is not a congress submission, not a late abstract, not a sponsorship request, not a registration request, not a TÜBİTAK application, not a TÜSEB proposal, not a Karadeniz Technical University proposal, not an intent declaration, not a meeting request, not a partner commitment, not a budget claim, and not a claim that Karadeniz Technical University, KTÜ Faculty of Medicine, the congress committee, TÜBİTAK, TÜSEB, TÜYZE, SEBİT, Acibadem CASE, Hacettepe, DEÜ, or any other organization supports this work.

## Why this route matters

The public KTÜ Faculty of Medicine page lists the 2026 Health Professionals Education in Artificial Intelligence and Simulation Congress and the faculty contact route. The linked congress page describes a health professions education event focused on artificial intelligence and simulation.

The route is valuable because the project needs a real health education surface, not only a generic AI safety repository. The event page names the transforming role of artificial intelligence and simulation in health professionals education and lists many accepted topics around AI, simulation, ethics, decision support, learning outcomes, and clinical education.
