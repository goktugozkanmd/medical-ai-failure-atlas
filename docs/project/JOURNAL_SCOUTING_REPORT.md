## Backup target — Journal of Biomedical Informatics

Reason:

JBI is indexed well and has a Special Communication category. It can work if the paper is made more methodological: a generalizable framework for converting clinical AI safety artifacts into governance evidence.

Main problem:

JBI’s official scope says papers should focus on a real-world biomedical or clinical problem, develop a novel approach, and evaluate it against current state-of-the-art. It also emphasizes method novelty and generalizable lessons. The current paper is closer to a governance evidence crosswalk than a new method paper.

Use only if:

- We add a stronger method section.
- We compare against generic medical benchmarks / model cards / compliance checklists.
- We show why failure-mode evidence adds something those do not.
